Data Sales

The Right Data
at Every Price

We offer three tiers of data — from fresh exclusives to cost-conscious aged sets — so you can match your investment to your strategy and margins.

Request a Data Sample
Most Popular
Exclusive Fresh Data
Straight from the feed, sold exclusively to you for the first 30 days. Delivered via Excel, Google Sheets, or API directly to your dialer or CRM. The highest-converting data set we offer — first position means first contact.
Semi-Exclusive Data
A cost-effective route to your target audience. Buyers are typically in second or third position. Delivered via Excel or CSV. Ideal for businesses with strong follow-up sequences and multi-touch marketing strategies.
Aged Data
Rested older data that may not have been contacted recently — making it a surprisingly productive and highly cost-conscious option. Third, fourth, or fifth position data available for volume-based outreach campaigns.
